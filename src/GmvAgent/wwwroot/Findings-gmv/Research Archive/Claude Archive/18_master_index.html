## NOTE ON PARLIAMENTARY EVIDENCE (URL 18)

URL: https://committees.parliament.uk/writtenevidence/106231/html/

This is the GMVML written evidence to Parliament (2022) on building safety. Key facts from this submission (referenced throughout the GMV legal report):

- GMVML manages 1,097 residential dwellings and 11 small commercial properties

- Divided into 10 high-rise and 6 low-rise apartment blocks

- All 10 high-rise blocks required EWS remediation

- Low-rise certification and non-cladding deficiencies remained problematic

- GMVML described as having no assets or income of its own, recovering costs from leaseholders

- GLA owns freehold; GMVML manages phases 1 & 2; GMVL still develops later phases
