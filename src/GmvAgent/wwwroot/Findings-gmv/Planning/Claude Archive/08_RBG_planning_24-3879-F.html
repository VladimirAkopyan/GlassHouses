TITLE: Planning Report — Greenwich Millennium Village Phases 1 & 2, West Parkside, Greenwich. Ref 24-3879-F

MEETING: Royal Borough of Greenwich Planning Board, 8 April 2025

NOTE: Direct PDF fetch blocked (PERMISSIONS_ERROR); content obtained via search results and Open Council Network.



DECISION:

Full planning permission GRANTED for the removal and replacement of combustible materials and build-ups in the existing external cladding materials on buildings within the original phases 1 & 2 of Greenwich Millennium Village.



BUILDINGS COVERED BY THIS APPLICATION (24-3879-F):

- Maurer Court

- Metcalfe Court

- Faraday Lodge

- Alamaro Lodge

- Da Vinci Lodge

- Newton Lodge

- Holly Court

- New Becquerel Court



(Note: Becquerel Court and Farnsworth Court were covered under a SEPARATE earlier application — referenced in the planning report as having been granted on either 19 March 2024 or 19 March 2025, with internal inconsistency noted in the report.)



APPLICANT: Greenwich Millennium Village Ltd



PROPOSED MATERIALS:

- Rendered cladding: Replaced with Sto Render Grain: Strukturmuster 1.0, colour-matched to existing render

- Timber cladding: Replaced with Cedral Click Cocoa Brown (fibre cement timber effect cladding board)

- All replacement materials: combustibility classification A1 or A2-s1,d0, in accordance with BS EN 13501-12



CONDITION AMENDMENT:

Condition 7 (Materials): Sto Render Grain to be colour-matched to the original vibrant colour palette on the Faraday, Alamaro & Newton blocks (Phase 2) to maintain original architectural design ideal.



INFORMATIVE ADDED:

"In respect of the Greenwich Millennium Village blocks Faraday Lodge, Alamaro Lodge & Maurer Court only, the applicant and the management company are advised to engage in discussions with residents to conduct an investigation into the feasibility of the cladding product Parklex Prodema GRCLAD-B as a potential alternative replacement material for the existing Cedar Wood cladding as suggested by residents."
