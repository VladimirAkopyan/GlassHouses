SITE HISTORY:

Phases 1 and 2 of Greenwich Millennium Village were built between 2002–2009, delivering:

- 1,095 homes

- 1,357 sqm of commercial and retail floorspace

- 5.8 acre Ecology Park

- 5.4 acre Southern Park



THE 2012 MASTERPLAN:

The masterplan for Phases 3, 4 and 5 was granted outline planning permission in 2012, providing:

- 1,746 new homes

- Up to 4,462 sqm of commercial (B1) floorspace

- Up to 1,190 sqm of retail floorspace (flexible A class uses A1–A4)

- A New Energy Centre

- New public squares and children's play areas

- New Ecology Swale

- Nursery, Community Centre, and Yacht Club Car Park



Work began on site in March 2013. To date (Feb 2017) delivered:

- 428 new homes, including 128 affordable homes (30% — 10% above the agreed requirement)



2017 YEAR AHEAD:

- Occupation of Plots 103 and 107 (Residential Buildings)

- Construction of Plots 101 and 102 (Residential Buildings) — 106 new homes, began January 2017

  - Mix of six-storey apartment buildings, three-storey family townhouses and two-storey courtyard houses

  - 44 of 106 homes designed for families

  - Expected ready: late 2018/early 2019

- Reserved Matters application for Plot 504 (Energy Centre)

- Reserved Matters application for Plot 201 (Landmark Residential Building — 20 storeys)

- Reserved Matters application for Plots 204 and 205 (Residential Buildings, ~100 new homes)

- Initial design development for the Ecology Swale (working with the Land Trust)

- Consultation on new Community Centre



PLOT 201 — LANDMARK RESIDENTIAL BUILDING:

- 20-storey landmark residential building

- 90 high quality new homes (mix of 1 and 2 bedrooms)

- New shared surface public route to the Ecology Park

- 100 sqm commercial unit at ground floor

- Communal roof terrace for residents at 18th floor

- Biodiversity roofs at 16th, 13th and 11th floors

- Materials: robust brickwork, coloured glass and contemporary zinc

- Footprint takes up only 64% of the parameter approved in outline planning permission

- Design inspired by varied barrel-vaulted roofline of original Erskine design (Phases 1 & 2)



ENERGY CENTRE — PLOT 504:

- New "heart" for Greenwich Millennium Village

- Combined Heat and Power (CHP) technology with thermal stores and high efficiency boilers and pumps

- Will deliver heating and hot water requirements for GMV via underground district heating pipe network

- Will also provide clean electricity to GMV

- Up to 16m high, built of brick with coloured stainless steel flue (rising to 26m)

- Illuminated Greenwich Millennium Village signage

- Hoped to bring into operation in 2018 (subject to planning)

- Features: 24hr/365-day operation; graphic lighting display showing energy generated
