TITLE: MD2574 Greenwich Peninsula Professional Fees

DECISION TYPE: Mayor

REFERENCE CODE: MD2574

DATE SIGNED: Tuesday 11 February 2020

DECISION BY: Sadiq Khan, Mayor of London



EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:

The regeneration of Greenwich Peninsula is a key priority for the Mayor and represents one of the largest development projects involving public land in London. The GLA (through its subsidiary GLA Land and Property Limited) is freeholder of the strategic landholding and is in two Development Agreements:

1. One with Knight Dragon Development Limited

2. One with Greenwich Millennium Village Limited (Taylor Wimpey and Countryside Properties)

Together, these developments will deliver a total of approximately 20,000 homes.



DECISION:

Mayor approves expenditure of £1,640,000 for professional fees at the Greenwich Peninsula until 2024/25 and corresponding expenditure on legal fees, development consultancy, valuation services and other specialist advice.



This brings total lifetime approval for professional fees to £6,607,163 for the period to end FY 2024/25.



KEY FACTS ON GMV:

- GMV is located to the south east of the Greenwich Peninsula, residential-led development

- Originally part of the Millennium Communities programme run by English Partnerships

- Initial phases completed in early 2000s

- Planning resumed on Phases 3, 4 & 5 after break due to market downturn and planning issues

- Most recent planning permission granted 2012 — permitted further 1,742 units

- Total GMV will deliver circa 3,000 homes

- 1,100 consented homes remained to be built (at time of this decision) subject to Reserved Matters



GLA GOVERNANCE:

- MD1111 authorised delegation of Mayor's decision-making powers re development decisions at Greenwich Peninsula to Deputy Mayor for Housing, Executive Director of Housing and Land, and Executive Director of Resources

- This was to provide clear separation between Mayor's planning decision making powers and his ability to make decisions affecting development



OUTSTANDING PROJECTS REQUIRING PROFESSIONAL FEES:

1. North Greenwich over-station development: £300,000

2. Final phase of Greenwich Millennium Village renegotiation: £140,000

Annual running costs: £240,000 per year (2020/21 through 2024/25)



EXPENDITURE BREAKDOWN BY YEAR:

- 2020/21: £240,000

- 2021/22: £240,000

- 2022/23: £240,000

- 2023/24: £240,000

- 2024/25: £240,000

- Over-station development (exceptional): £300,000

- GMV final phase (exceptional, 2020/21): £140,000

Total: £1,640,000
